Gobi Manchurian

Description:

A fried cauliflower Indo Chinese spicy dish.

Ingredients:

Fried Cauliflower
  • oil for deep frying
  • 5 table spoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 table spoons cornmeal
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 15 cauliflower florets
Manchurian Sauce:
  • 3/4 cup vegetable stock, divided
  • 1 tablespoon cornmeal
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon chile-garlic sauce (such as Sriracha)
  • salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1 green chile pepper, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon finely chopped ginger
  • 1 green onion, chopped, or to taste (Optional)

Steps:

  1. Step 1: Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Step 2: Mix flour, cornmeal, water, black pepper, and salt together in a bowl to make a thick batter. Toss and mix cauliflower florets until they're fully coated.
  3. Step 3: Deep fry a few cauliflower florets at a time until they're golden brown, roughly 3 minutes. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate.
  4. Step 4: Mix 1/4 cup vegetable stock with cornmeal in a bowl. Add soy sauce, ketchup, chile-garlic sauce, and salt.
  5. Step 5: Heat oil in a wok or frying pan over high heat. Add onions, chile pepper, garlic, and ginger and stir fry for a few seconds. Add cornmeal mixture and cook 1 to 2 minutes. Add remaining vegetable stock and cook until very thick, about 10 minutes. Remove sauce from heat.
  6. Step 6: Spoon sauce over fried cauliflower and toss slightly. Garnish with green onions and serve.

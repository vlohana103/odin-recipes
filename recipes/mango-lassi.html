Mango Lassi

An Indian mango yogurt based drink that is smooth, creamy, and amazing!

Ingredients:

  • 2 manos- peeled, seeded, and diced
  • 2 cups of plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup ice

Steps:

  1. Step 1: In a blender, combine mangos, yogurt, sugar, and ice. Blend until smooth.
  2. Step 2:Pour into glass and serve.
